Constructed from 1887–89 as the entrance to the 1889 World's Fair,
it was initially criticized by some of France's leading artists and intellectuals for its design,
but it has become a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognisable
structures in the world.[3] The Eiffel Tower is the most-visited paid monument in the world;
6.91 million people ascended it in 2015.
